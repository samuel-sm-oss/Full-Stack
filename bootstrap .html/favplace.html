The Chola dynasty

Big Temple

The Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, celebrated for its towering vimana and exceptional Chola dynasty craftsmanship, making it an architectural masterpiece and cultural treasure.

Gangai Konda Cholapuram

Gangaikonda Cholapuram, an ancient temple town in Tamil Nadu, is renowned for its magnificent Gangaikonda Cholesvara Temple, built by the Chola emperor Rajendra I, featuring a towering vimana and intricate Chola architecture, making it a remarkable historical and architectural site.

Thiruvarur Thiyagaraja Temple

Thiruvarur Kovil, also known as Thyagaraja Temple, is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Thyagaraja, and it is located in Thiruvarur, Tamil Nadu, known for its grand architecture and as a significant center for Carnatic music and culture.
